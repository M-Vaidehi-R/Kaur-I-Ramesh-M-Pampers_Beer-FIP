How we brew




We brew Pampers Beer with 100% pure ingredients: furits and water. Going through the phases of cooking, cooling, fermentation and lagering. Water is mixed with the pulp of fruits. This creates wort, a sweet sugary liquid, which is then boiled. We take 28 days to brew Pampers Beer. To get the perfectly balanced taste, refreshing clarity and beautiful golden-yellow colour. Quality takes time, it's worth it.

About

Pampers Beer which was established in Jan 2022, is the first brand to introduce non-alcoholic beer for Mothers as well as Babies. Pregnant women have some cravings of drinking beer but they can't have a regular beer but we've come up with the best solution! Also, Babies can't drink was a thing of past, now it's the time to let them taste this beer with 0% alcohol! We have designed baby beer in baby bottle (feeding bottle), making it way easier to consume. As said.....WHY SHOULD PARENTS HAVE ALL THE FUN..

Quality and Safety

We brew for the forthcoming so quality and safety of the product is our responsibility. This is why we brew in a clean brewery and always use fresh ingredients to make it. We make sure that we're serving healthy and the best beer. While brewing, proper steps are taken by everyone working in the brewery to stay hygienic and brew fresh. Sanitized stations and packing areas, 95% score in quality checking, proper measures against Covid-19...trust us..for the best non-alcoholic beer.
